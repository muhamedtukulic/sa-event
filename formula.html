Bijelo Dugme


Bijelo dugme is one of the most famous rock groups of the former Yugoslavia, founded in Sarajevo in 1974. Although young, the members of the group were already experienced musicians. The band was active from 1974 to 1989, and during that period they released about fifteen albums, along with several compilations and numerous singles. At the beginning of 2005, the reunion of Bijelo Dugmet was announced and in June they held only three comeback concerts, in Sarajevo, Zagreb and Belgrade in front of a total of more than two hundred thousand spectators. The group didn't get together again after that.
